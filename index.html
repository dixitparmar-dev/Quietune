What is Quietune?

Quietune is a peaceful and beautifully crafted music player made for those who love a clean and focused way to enjoy their music. With a unique design and a calm experience, it lets you listen without distractions, noise, or clutter. No ads, no chaos — just your music, your moment. Quietune isn’t trying to be the biggest, just something truly different.

Features

unique ui experience

Clean and minimal design that keeps the focus on your music. No clutter, no distractions — just a smooth and calming interface that feels easy to use.

Playlist & Search

Find your music easily and create playlists in an interface designed for clarity and calmness.

Smart Day / Night Mode

Adapts to your system’s theme based on time, ensuring a calm experience day or night.

Keyboard Shortcuts & Volume

Take control of your playback and volume with seamless keyboard shortcut support.

Shuffle & Infinite Play

Play your songs endlessly or randomly — quietly tailored to your flow.
